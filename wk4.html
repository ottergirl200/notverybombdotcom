Yayoi Kusama is a contemporary Japanese artists who is most notable for her immersive sculpture and installation works. There is a heavy motif of dots throughout her oeurve, the paintings being called "Infinity Nets" and the rooms "Infinity Rooms." Her artworks express an algorithm-like fashion, especially when considering the sensory interactions involved.
